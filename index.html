About

Why this exists

India produces some of the most detailed government data in the world — NCRB, Census, NFHS, ECI, MOSPI. It is also notoriously hard to access, scattered across PDFs, poorly indexed, and rarely visualised in one place.

India Data Lens is a personal project to change that. Each page takes one dataset, extracts the numbers, and builds an interactive visual that makes the data readable and shareable.

Tamil Nadu is a special focus — it is consistently both a data-rich state and an outlier in many national statistics.

🏛️
Primary sources only
NCRB, Census, ECI, NFHS. No aggregators. Direct from government publications.
🗺️
State-level depth
National averages hide state-level reality. Every dataset goes down to district level where possible.
📂
Data included
Every page ships with the underlying CSV files so you can download and use the data yourself.
🔗
Fully cited
Every number links back to the source PDF, table number, and publication year.
