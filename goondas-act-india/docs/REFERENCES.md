# References — Goondas Act in India: Data Sources

## Primary Official Sources

### 1. NCRB — National Crime Records Bureau

**Crime in India (Annual Report)**
- Publisher: Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India
- Coverage: 2017 onwards (preventive detention chapter)
- Relevant chapter: Chapter on Preventive Detentions (act-wise, state/UT-wise table)
- Download: https://ncrb.gov.in/en/crime-in-india-year-wise.html
- Key URLs:
  - CII 2022 Vol I: https://www.ncrb.gov.in/uploads/nationalcrimerecordsbureau/custom/1701607577CrimeinIndia2022Book1.pdf
  - CII 2023 Part I: https://www.ncrb.gov.in/uploads/files/1CrimeinIndia2023PartI.pdf
  - CII 2020 Vol III: https://www.ncrb.gov.in/uploads/nationalcrimerecordsbureau/custom/1653724294_CII2020%20Volume%203.pdf

**Prison Statistics India (PSI)**
- Publisher: NCRB / Ministry of Home Affairs
- Coverage: 1995 onwards; latest published = 2022
- Relevant tables: Detenus in prison by state, demographics (age, education, caste, religion)
- Download: https://ncrb.gov.in/en/prison-statistics-india
- Key URLs:
  - PSI 2022: https://www.ncrb.gov.in/uploads/nationalcrimerecordsbureau/custom/psiyearwise2022/1701613297PSI2022ason01122023.pdf
  - PSI 2021 Additional Tables: https://ncrb.gov.in/sites/default/files/PSI-2021/PSI2021-AddTables.html

### 2. TN SCRB — Tamil Nadu State Crime Records Bureau

**Crime Review (Annual Publication)**
- Publisher: Tamil Nadu State Crime Records Bureau
- Coverage: TN-specific; district-wise and category-wise Goondas Act data
- Website: https://scrb.tn.gov.in
- Note: District-wise and category-wise breakdowns not in public domain; request via RTI or direct SCRB contact

### 3. Open Government Data Platform India

**Crime in India — 2022 Dataset**
- URL: https://www.data.gov.in/catalog/crime-india-2022
- Format: Downloadable tables (CSV/XLS)
- Note: Covers cognizable crimes; preventive detention tables may be in SLL section

## Secondary / Research Sources

### 4. Thakur Foundation Research Report (Most Comprehensive Compiled Source)

- Title: *Report on Preventive Detentions under the Tamil Nadu Prevention Of Dangerous Activities Of Bootleggers, Cyber Law Offenders, Drug Offenders, Forest-Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders, Sand Offenders, Sexual Offenders, Slum-Grabbers And Video Pirates Act, 1982*
- Author: Akila R S (Advocate and independent researcher, Chennai)
- Funder: Thakur Family Foundation Inc.
- PDF URL: https://www.thakur-foundation.org/upload/judgements/1649834297_Akila%20RS,%20Preventive%20Detentions%20under%20Tamil%20Nadu%20Goondas%20Act%20(1).pdf
- Data coverage: 2016–2021; includes NCRB PSI data, TN SCRB RTI data, district-wise figures, age/education/caste/religion profile of detenus
- Key data in Annexure X of the report

### 5. Constitutional Law Blog — Preventive Detention Analysis

- Title: *Preventive Detention — Constitutional Law and Philosophy*
- URL: https://indconlawphil.wordpress.com/tag/preventive-detention/
- Cited data: TN SCRB Crime Review 2021 (3,194 detained; 2,843 under Goondas Act = 89%); habeas corpus analysis (95% of 7,448 cases from Goondas Act, 2000–2022)

### 6. Madras High Court Judgments

- Platform: indiankanoon.org (free, searchable)
- Key case reference: Madras HC November 14 judgment citing NCRB — TN = 51.2% of all detentions nationally
- Also cited by Supreme Court in Savukku Shankar detention case (August 2024)

## RTI Filing Guidance

For district-level or category-wise data not in published reports:
- Portal: https://rtionline.gov.in
- File with: State Home Department, District Collector, or Commissioner of Police
- Specifically request: District-wise preventive detentions, category-wise breakdown (bootlegger / goonda / drug offender), year-wise trend
