Primary sources

All data is from government publications or peer-reviewed research. See docs/REFERENCES.md in this repository for full citation details.

  • 1 NCRB — Crime in India (Annual Report, 2017–2023)
    Preventive detention chapter: state/UT-wise, act-wise tables. Ministry of Home Affairs, GoI. ncrb.gov.in → Publications → Crime in India
  • 2 NCRB — Prison Statistics India (PSI, 1995–2022)
    Detenus in prison by state; age, education, caste, religion profiles. ncrb.gov.in → Publications → Prison Statistics India
  • 3 TN SCRB — Crime Review (Annual)
    Tamil Nadu-specific; district-wise and category-wise Goondas Act data. scrb.tn.gov.in
  • 4 Akila R S (2022). Report on Preventive Detentions under TN Goondas Act.
    Thakur Family Foundation. Most comprehensive compiled source (NCRB + SCRB + RTI). Download PDF — Thakur Foundation
  • 5 Open Government Data Platform India — Crime in India 2022 data.gov.in → Crime in India 2022
  • 6 Madras High Court, November 2022 (unreported)
    Cited NCRB PSI data; confirmed TN = 51.2% of all preventive detenus in India. Searchable via indiankanoon.org
  • 7 Supreme Court of India — Savukku Shankar detention case (August 2024)
    Counsel cited TN = 51% of national preventive detentions before a 3-judge bench. ETV Bharat report (Aug 30, 2024)
